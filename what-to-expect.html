What to expect

The ultimate open-fire celebration lands at Yas Island this November

Chefs

  • Masterclasses

    Get hands-on with BBQ like never before. Join live, interactive masterclasses hosted by celebrity chefs and pitmasters. Learn grilling techniques, recipes, and pro tips straight from the legends themselves. Whether you're a beginner or a backyard pro, there’s something for everyone.

  • Eating Competitions

    Ready to take the heat? Step up for adrenaline-pumping BBQ eating contests happening throughout the weekend. Compete for bragging rights, surprise goodies, and that ultimate food lover’s glory.

  • The BBQ Experience

    From smoke and sizzle to bold flavor – this is the ultimate fire-fueled festival. Expect live fire zones, sizzling bites from 25+ vendors, and grilling legends cooking over blazing flames. Sip on craft cocktails, sample diverse cuisines, and explore our curated Market Zone packed with foodie finds.

  • Entertainment & Music

    Catch high-energy regional acts, live bands, DJ sets, and nonstop entertainment from day to night. The main stage will be buzzing with excitement, featuring group activities, headline performances, and exclusive meet & greets with our celebrity chefs.

  • For the Little Foodies

    With two fully dedicated Kids’ Zones, we’ve got the little ones covered. Expect hands-on fun, BBQ-themed games, interactive play zones, and lots of smiles. A safe, joyful space for kids to have a blast while you enjoy the smoke and soul.

  • VIP Zone

    Upgrade your experience with fast-track entry, access to private lounges, premium seating, and elevated vibes. Whether you’re here to relax or network, our VIP zones offer the perfect escape.

  • Event Zones

    From the Chill Zone to the Open Fire Stage, every corner of the festival offers something unique. Wander through the F&B lanes, shop exclusive merchandise in the Market Zone, and don’t miss the action at our masterclass and performance stages.
