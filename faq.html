Frequently Asked Questions

What is open fire food festival?

The Open Fire Food Festival is a fiery celebration of bold flavors, cultural diversity, and the primal art of cooking over open flames.

Inspired by Steve Harvey’s passion for fire-grilled food, this two-day festival brings together the world’s top celebrity BBQ chefs and pitmasters for a one-of-a-kind culinary experience.

When is the open fire food festival?

The festival runs from November 8th - 9th, 2025 at Etihad Park, Yas Island, Abu Dhabi.

When is the open fire food festival?

The festival takes place at Etihad Park, Yas Island, Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. Click here for directions.

Will there be free parking?

Yes! Free parking is available right in front of the festival grounds, within walking distance of the entrance. Please see directions for exact details.

Is It a free event?

No, the Open Fire Food Festival is a ticketed event. You can explore different ticket options on our tickets page.

Can I buy a ticket at the door?

Yes, tickets are available at the entrance on the day of the event, subject to availability.

What Is the dress code?

There is no strict dress code, but we recommend dressing comfortably and in accordance with local culture.

Is It a family-friendly event?

Absolutely! The festival is designed for all ages. However, there is a strict no-pets policy, so please leave pets at home.

Is there a lost and found?

Yes. Our staff at the information booths will assist you with lost belongings.

What should I do if I notice a security or safety issue?

Please report any safety or security concerns to the nearest information booth. Onsite security staff are ready to assist immediately.

How do I lodge a complaint or provide feedback?

Your experience matters to us! Send your feedback via a DM on our Instagram account, and our team will ensure it reaches the right department promptly.

Who will be performing at the festival?

All live performances and cooking demonstrations will take place on the Main Stage.

How to participate in the event?

You can participate by purchasing tickets through our tickets page. Whether you want to enjoy the food, attend live cooking demos, or join interactive workshops, your ticket gives you access to all the festival activities.

Is the event pet friendly?

No, the Open Fire Food Festival is not pet-friendly. Please leave pets at home to ensure the comfort and safety of all attendees.
